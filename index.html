What you'll see in the demo

  • Inventory checklist with item photos (example Millennium item included).
  • Node workflow: enter node number, then work one node at a time.
  • Splice locations: GPS capture + photo + timestamp required before "Complete".
  • Units threshold alert: when used units approach allowed units, PRIME gets warned.
  • Pricing privacy: prices are hidden from SPLICERS (and can be hidden between other parties via RLS).
Tip: Once Supabase is wired, this becomes real: files upload to Storage, rows land in Postgres, and policies enforce who can see what.
